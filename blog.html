Registration Process

● All the paper must be submitted to Easy Chair (Submit Your Paper)
● Once your paper is accepted the registration process begins.
● It is mandatory for at least one author (of an accepted paper) to get registered himself.
● The candidate is required to submit the scanned copy of the dully filled form (Download Registration Form)
● Research Scholars/Students have to send copy of their Institute’s ID-Card along with the registration form.
● Authors with multiple papers have to register separately for each paper.
● Send scanned copy of your completed registration form, copyright agreement form along with the scanned copy of the proof of payments to Updatesoon@vitap.ac.in
