Submission Guidelines

Full papers must be original and novel research
contributions with evalutation results and are limited to six(5)
printed pages (10-point font) including figures, references and appendices (two
additional pages are allowed with a per page extra fee). Short papers, limited
in length to four (4) pages, should be more visonary in nature and are meant to
discuss new challenges and visions, showcase early research results and
explore novel research directions. All the manuscript should be submitted using easy
chair submission link in PDF format without author name and affiliation as per the
Springer Format.

Authors should ensure that the similarity score of
their research paper is not above 10 %. Manuscripts having similarity score more than 10 %
shall not be processed.

Review Process & Oral Presentation:

  • The paper having the similarity score less than 10 % shall be processed for double blind review.
  • The papers will be peer reviewed by technical committee of the conference.
  • Based on review the authors are informed about their paper status:

    • (a) Oral presentation for accepted paper
    (b) Accepted with minor corrections for Oral Presentation
    (c) Rejected.
